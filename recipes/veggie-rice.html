Flavorful Vegetable Fried Rice

Description

This vegetable fried rice is a quick and easy meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. Packed with colorful veggies and seasoned with soy sauce and sesame oil, it's both nutritious and delicious. Serve it as a main dish or as a side to your favorite Asian-inspired meals.

The recipe is versatile, allowing you to use any vegetables you have on hand. It's a great way to use up leftover rice and makes for a satisfying, wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup corn
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the onions and garlic, and saut√© until the onions are translucent.
  2. Add the carrots, bell peppers, peas, and corn. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty side of the skillet. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix them with the vegetables.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  5. Pour the soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice and vegetables. Stir-fry for another 3-5 minutes, ensuring everything is well coated with the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving. Enjoy your vegetable fried rice!
