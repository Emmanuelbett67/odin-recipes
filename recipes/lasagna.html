Delicious Homemade Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food. Layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella make this dish a family favorite. Perfect for a cozy dinner or special occasions, this lasagna is sure to impress.

The recipe combines traditional Italian flavors with a simple preparation method. Whether you're a seasoned cook or a beginner, you'll find this lasagna easy to make and delicious to eat.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 jars marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef, onion, and garlic. Cook until the beef is browned and the onion is tender. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers twice, ending with a layer of meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
